Introduction

Beekeeping, a delicate blend of agriculture and ecology, confronts challenges in traditional hive management methods. The Smart Beehive Monitoring System seeks to transform beekeeping by integrating advanced technologies like cameras and artificial intelligence.
Our focus includes early detection of abscondment and swift identification of feeding irregularities. By simplifying complex processes, our system empowers beekeepers with timely alerts and actionable insights, promoting the overall health and sustainability of their colonies.
